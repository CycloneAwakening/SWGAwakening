Welcome to SWG Awakening
Setup Complete

While your game downloads, here are some tips and tricks to help you get started on SWG Awakening

Account Creation

Don't forget to create a forum and game account. Then use those details to login to the game.

Community

General Chat: You can chat with other players in-game no matter where you are. On your chat box, select the "General" tab.

Auction Chat: Check the "Auction" channel in game to see where players are buffing and selling their goods. Right click a chat tab and add or modify a chat tab to include the "Auction" channel.

SWG Options - Read online

Use the "SWG Options" button in the launcher to configure your game.

Graphics: Set your Game Resolution to match your monitor's resolution, then set Vertex/Pixel Shader to "2.0 (override)"" and check "Use Safe Renderer". It is also recommended to run the game in both "Windowed Mode" and "Borderless Window" mode.

Sound: Make sure "Disable Audio" is NOT selected.

Game: Check "Skip Intro Sequence" and if your computer performs well, "Disabled Character System LoD Manager."

Advanced: Check "Disable World Preloading" and "Disabled File Caching."

Game Play Suggestions

When you start on SWG Awakening, after first creating your character, join up to general chat and have a chat with the other players. Don't forget to migrate your stats before leaving the station!

There is a 30 minute timer on creating and deleting characters. If you create or delete a character, you will not be able to create a new one for 30 minutes.

Buff Droids are located in Coronet and Mos Eisley outside the starport (as well as other locations), use these for free starter buffs with short duration. It is highly recommend you check the auction channel or ask in general chat to see who is buffing.

When first starting your leveling journey, a good way to earn XP is to kill the low difficulty spawns around the NPC cities.

To read more useful information about getting started on SWG Awakening take a look at this player written guide.
